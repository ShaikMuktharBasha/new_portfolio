# Certification Logos

Place your certification logo images in this folder with the following exact names:

## Required Image Files:

1. **aviatrix-logo.png** - For Aviatrix Multicloud Network Associate
2. **mongodb-logo.png** - For MongoDB Associate Developer  
3. **redhat-logo.png** - For Red Hat Learner
4. **salesforce-logo.png** - For Salesforce Certified AI Associate

## Image Requirements:

- **Format**: PNG (preferred) or JPG
- **Size**: Recommended 200x200 pixels or higher
- **Background**: Transparent PNG preferred, or white background
- **Quality**: High resolution for crisp display

## Where to get logos:

1. **Official Company Websites**: Most companies provide logo downloads
2. **Credly Badges**: You can save logo images from your Credly badge pages
3. **Company Brand Guidelines**: Check official brand asset pages

## Tips:

- Use square aspect ratio images for best results
- Ensure logos are official company logos
- High contrast logos work best
- Test the logos after uploading to see how they look

Once you place the logo files here, refresh your portfolio page to see them!
